Sharon An

PROJECT DESIGNER

ASSOC. AIA, LEED GREEN ASSOC.

BFA IN ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN, MINOR IN GRAPHIC DESIGN, MARYLAND INSTITUTE COLLEGE OF ART

MFA.ARCH CANDIDATE, VIRGINIA TECH

Sharon is involved in all phases of architectural design and production. She also works with architectural graphics, generating a wide variety of visuals for design. She is greatly involved in the design effort required to present new projects to the city and neighborhood meetings. Sharon is working to obtain accreditation as a registered architect.

As a graduate from Maryland Institute College of Art, Sharon has brought her interdisciplinary expertise in art and design to Urban Design Group's process and culture. Her personal design projects dealt with revitalization of various neighborhoods of the Baltimore city, which informed her growing knowledge and awareness of the city's physical and social construct.

Outside of work, she loves to explore around cafes, ice cream shops, and all things outdoors.
