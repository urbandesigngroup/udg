M.ARCH WITH CONCENTRATION IN URBAN DESIGN CATHOLIC UNIVERSITY

Chris has experience working on a wide array of project types, from small buildings to master plans, renovation and new construction, residential and commercial developments, public and private sector projects. Prior to his arrival at Urban Design Group, he has worked with notable clients such as the U.S. Department of State, National Parks Service and JBG Smith. He is currently working to obtain accreditation as a registered architect. Before working in the AEC industry, he was a defense contractor providing maintenance services for intelligence, reconnaissance and surveillance (ISR) systems for multiple Army, Navy and Marine units staged domestically and deployed worldwide. In his spare time, he enjoys photography, biking, basketball and curating his collection of Transformers.
